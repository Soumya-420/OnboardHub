## ‚ú® New Features

### 1. Mentor Readiness Gauge
A new metric that assesses the responsiveness and activity of repository maintainers. This helps students pick project where they actually get feedback!
- **Algorithm:** Calculates Star/Issue ratio, presence of triage templates, and active health checks.
- **Visuals:** Integrated into the "Top Stats Power Grid" with a sleek animated gauge.

### 2. Smart Proposal Drafter (WoC 5.0)
Students can now draft professional hackathon proposals in seconds.
- **Markdown Output:** Includes technical approach, timeline, and repository-specific context.
- **One-Click Copy:** Ready to be pasted into the project application.

### 3. Community Bonding Toolkit
Ensures students don't miss the critical "bonding phase" of the hackathon.
- **Auto-Detection:** Automatically scans for Discord and Slack links.
- **Talking Points:** Suggested conversation starters and questions for maintainer sync-ups.

### 4. Candidate Compatibility Profiler (Flagship Feature)
The "mic-drop" feature for WoC 5.0. It bridges the gap between a student's skills and a project's needs.
- **Skill Matching:** Input your expertise (e.g., React, TypeScript) and the app calculates a **Compatibility Score** for every issue.
- **Tailored Proposals:** The generated proposal now includes a "Why I'm a Fit" section based on your specific skills.
- **Visual Feedback:** Color-coded progress bars show exactly how well you match an issue's requirements.

### 5. The "Delight" Factor
- **Confetti Celebration:** A visual reward when you copy your proposal, keeping morale high during long hackathon sessions. ‚ú®

### 6. Search History & UI Polish
- **Recently Analyzed:** Quick access to previous repos.
- **Premium Grid:** Restructured the dashboard for maximum clarity and "WOW" factor.
